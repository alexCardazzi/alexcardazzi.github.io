    Places I’ve Lived

    I was born in Queens, New York, but was raised in Rockland County, New York. I did my undergraduate degree at Ramapo College in Bergen County, New Jersey. From there, I went on to study at West Virginia University in Monongalia County for my PhD. Currently, I reside in Norfolk City, Virginia where I am an assistant professor in the Economics Department of Old Dominion University. See below for a walking tour of places I’ve spent a significant amount of time living.
